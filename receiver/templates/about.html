Who are we?


Hello Reader, we hope that the main aim of you visiting our website is fulfilled, and believe that our website could be of small help to you.
If you have contributed to the resources section, then we are in debt of you and are more than thankful. We are a group of students, who
came up with this hope of helping others through a website and our only motive is to act on our side to provide to our citizens
of India in these tough times.

Our Aim


Concerned with the drastic increase of Covid-19 Cases in India, we are planning to make a little contribution from our side to help people in need. As we can see, Covid Patients are going out of Remdesivir Injections, Beds, Oxygen Tanks, Blood & Plasma and there is no central hub where people can reach out to for any help.

All the information and helps available are scattered on social media platforms or other sources, in case if an individual needs a specific help, there is no such medium where one can provide information which will ensure that one might get the necessary help. So to overcome this problem we are developing a website that will contain all the states and their regions bifurcated with details of places where one can get those injections, beds, blood/plasma, etc.

If anyone is willing to help, the person can add their details. In case anyone is in urgent need, they can reach out to people via this platform.
