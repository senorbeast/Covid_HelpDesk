About Us

Who are we?


We are a group of students, who came up with the hope of helping others through this website and our only motive is to act on our side to provide to citizens of India in these adverse times. We hope that the main aim of you visiting our website is fulfilled, and believe that our website could be of small help to you. If you have contributed to the resources section, then we are in debt to you and are more than thankful.

Our Aim


Concerned with the drastic increase in Covid-19 Cases in India, Covid Patients are searching for Beds, Oxygen Tanks, Blood & Plasma and there is no central hub where people can reach out to for any help.

if an individual needs a specific help, all the information and helps available are scattered on social media platforms or other sources, in case. To overcome this problem we made this website to ensure that we can gather the required data which can be accessed in one place.
If anyone is willing to help, the person can add their details. In case anyone is in urgent need, they can reach out to people via this platform.
