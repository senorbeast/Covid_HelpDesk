About Us

Who are we?


We are a group of students, who came up with the hope of helping others through this website and our only motive is to act on our side to help the citizens of India in these adverse times. We hope that the main aim of you visiting our website is fulfilled, and believe that our website could be of some help to you if yes, please write your views in the feedback section. Also, if you have contributed to the resources section, then we are extremely indebted and more than thankful.

Our Aim


Concerned with the drastic increase in Covid-19 Cases and deaths in India, Covid Patients are in search of Beds, Oxygen Tanks, Blood & Plasma and there is no central hub where people can reach out for any help. The one in search of these resources fails to get them due to the lack of updated data and a lot of data that is scattered over the social media platforms and other sources which cannot be easily accessed. To overcome this problem we made this website to ensure that we can gather the required data which can be accessed by the needy in one place. We aim to expand our network and help people with their requirements as soon as possible and also provide as much relevant data as possible. If you want to join our network you can reach out to us at our Email ID provided below.
